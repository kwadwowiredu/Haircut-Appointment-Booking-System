About Britain Haircut

Britain Haircut was founded in 2010 by CEO and master barber, Frank Obiri Yeboah. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, Mr.Yeboah had a vision to create a modern, welcoming barbershop that would provide top-notch service and expertise to the community.

What started as a small, one-chair shop has now grown into a bustling barbershop with a team of skilled barbers, serving hundreds of loyal customers each week. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and passion for the craft have earned us a reputation as one of the premier barbershops in the city.

Meet the CEO

Frank Obiri Yeboah, CEO and Master Barber

Mr. Obiri Yeboah has been a barber for over 20 years, honing his skills and developing a deep passion for the craft. He founded Britain Haircut with the goal of creating a modern, welcoming barbershop that would provide exceptional service and an unparalleled grooming experience.
